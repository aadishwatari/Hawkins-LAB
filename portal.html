// HAWKINS LAB 1984
HP: ❤❤❤

HOW TO PLAY:

* MOVEMENT: Use [W, A, S, D] or the [ARROW KEYS] to navigate the labyrinth.
* COMBAT: Press [ENTER] when close to the Demogorgon to perform a melee stun. This knocks it out temporarily.
* SAFE ZONES: The Experimental Lab is a designated safe zone. The Demogorgon cannot track or hunt you while you stay inside.
* OBJECTIVE: Avoid the Demogorgon and step onto doors or portals to instantly access other rooms and areas. Escape through the Main Door on the far left.
[WASD/ARROWS] Move | [ENTER] Melee Stun Demogorgon
